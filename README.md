# Welcome
Welcome to Team Iron's class project site for **MingleMap**, a concept for helping people explore nearby spaces, signal when they're open to meeting new people, and connect in person with confidence.

## Project Overview
This website showcases our course work and deliverables for MingleMap. It explains the problem of modern social isolation, outlines our proposed solution, and collects the presentations, labs, and reference material produced by the team.

## Pages at a Glance
- **Home:** Introduces the social isolation problem, describes how MingleMap bridges nearby people and places, and highlights key user stories for the app.
- **The Team:** Profiles each teammate with their role, photo, and a short bio.
- **Presentations:** Hosts embedded slide decks and downloads for the final demo, feasibility, and design presentations, plus the Lab 1 outline.
- **Labs:** Links to individual and group lab submissions, including PDFs and the GitHub wiki entry.
- **Deliverables:** Collects weekly status reports, process flow diagrams, algorithm visuals, risk assessments, and user stories.
- **References:** Lists the sources, articles, and tools that informed the project, along with a glossary graphic.
- **Poster:** Reserved space for the final project poster.
